The Conference on Type Design and Technology

8+9 March 2012

The Robothon 2012 is over. Thanks everyone for coming!

The Royal Academy of Arts hosts the 2012 edition of the Robothon Conference on Type Design and Technology. This year we’re proud to announce an international range of speakers, the new UFO 3 format, amazing new tools and ideas. Robothon is a great opportunity to hang out with your colleagues from around Europe, meet the Type & Media crew, and see the Gerrit Noordzij Prize exhibition and seminar.
