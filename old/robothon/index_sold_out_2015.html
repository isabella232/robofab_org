The master Type & Media of the Royal Academy of Art (KABK) in conjunction with partners has organised the 5th edition of Robothon on Thursday 5 and Friday 6 March in The Hague.

#robothon15

Sold out

Robothon is the triennial conference on font software and font technology, with speakers and audience from all over the globe. The programme consists of a workshop and two days of lectures and presentations.

Confirmed speakers are: Petr van Blokland (Buro PvB&CM), Frank Grie√ühammer (Adobe), Miguel Sousa (Adobe), Rainer Erich Scheichelbauer (Glyphs), Andy Clymer (Hoefler&Co), Jack Jennings (Mechanic), Frederik Berlaen (RoboFont), Erik van Blokland (Superpolator), Tal Leming (TypeSupply), Jeremie Hornus (TypoSansPlomb), Just van Rossum (Python workshop)

Friday afternoon brings the opening of the Karel Martens exhibition, and the new Gerrit Noordzij Prize winner Cyrus Highsmith.
